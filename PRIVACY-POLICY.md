## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data in these limited circumstances:

**Service Providers:**
- Cloud hosting (Vercel, Supabase)
- Authentication (Clerk)
- AI processing (Anthropic)
- Email services (Resend)
- Analytics tools

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

**Booking Partners:**
- When you click a booking link, you're redirected to third-party sites (Kiwi.com, Booking.com, etc.)
- Those sites have their own privacy policies
- We may receive a commission confirmation but NOT your payment details

**Legal Requirements:**
- We may disclose data if required by law
- To comply with subpoenas or court orders
- To protect our rights or safety
- To investigate fraud or abuse

**Business Transfers:**
- If Keffy is acquired or merged, your data may transfer to the new owner
- We will notify you before your data is transferred or becomes subject to a different privacy policy

**Anonymized Data:**
- We may share aggregated, anonymized statistics that cannot identify you
- Example: "70% of users prefer beach destinations"

## 5. Data Retention

**Active Accounts:**
- We retain your data as long as your account is active
- Conversation history is kept to provide continuous service

**Deleted Accounts:**
- Most data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- Some data may be retained longer for legal/business purposes:
  - Backup copies (90 days)
  - Financial records (7 years, as required by law)
  - Anonymized usage data (indefinitely)

**Conversation Soft-Delete:**
- When you delete a conversation, it's hidden from your view
- For quality assurance, we retain conversations for 90 days
- After 90 days, conversations are permanently deleted

**Legal Holds:**
- Data subject to legal holds or investigations may be retained longer
